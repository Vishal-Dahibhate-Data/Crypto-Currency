# ₿ Cryptocurrency Market Analysis Dashboard

## Interactive Cryptocurrency Analytics Using Power BI, SQL & Excel

A comprehensive Business Intelligence project that analyzes cryptocurrency market performance across multiple digital assets. The dashboard provides insights into market capitalization, trading volume, price movements, and historical trends, enabling investors and analysts to make data-driven decisions.

## 📌 Project Overview

The Cryptocurrency Market Analysis Dashboard is designed to explore and visualize historical cryptocurrency data from 2013 to 2021. The dashboard enables users to analyze market trends, compare digital assets, monitor price fluctuations, and evaluate key performance indicators such as market capitalization, trading volume, opening and closing prices, and daily highs and lows.

The project focuses on transforming raw cryptocurrency data into actionable insights through interactive Power BI visualizations and advanced analytics.

## 🎯 Business Problem

The cryptocurrency market is highly volatile and generates large volumes of financial data daily. Investors and analysts require efficient tools to monitor market trends and identify patterns that influence investment decisions.

This project addresses the following questions:

* How have cryptocurrency prices evolved over time?
* Which cryptocurrencies have the highest market capitalization?
* How does trading volume impact market performance?
* What trends exist in opening, closing, high, and low prices?
* How do different cryptocurrencies compare across years?

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze historical cryptocurrency market performance.
* Compare major cryptocurrencies across multiple years.
* Track market capitalization and trading volume trends.
* Evaluate price volatility using High, Low, Open, and Close values.
* Identify growth patterns and market cycles.
* Build interactive dashboards for financial analysis and decision-making.
